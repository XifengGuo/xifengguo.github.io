Research Interests

My interests were on Pattern Recognition and Image Processing, especially on Fingerprint Recognition before 2016.



Now I'm interested in Machine Learning and Computer Vision. To be specific, my study focuses on unsupervised transfer learning and clustering using deep neural networks.
